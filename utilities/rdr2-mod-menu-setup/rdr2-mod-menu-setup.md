**Use at your own risk. For research/educational purposes only. Mod menus can violate game terms of service and may result in bans or other consequences.**

---

### Step 1 – Download Required Tools

Download the following:

* HorseMenu: [https://github.com/YimMenu/HorseMenu](https://github.com/YimMenu/HorseMenu)
* Fate Injector: [https://github.com/fligger/FateInjector/releases/tag/1.0](https://github.com/fligger/FateInjector/releases/tag/1.0)

---

### Step 2 – Run Injector

Launch **Fate Injector** before starting the game.

---

### Step 3 – Launch Game

Start **Red Dead Redemption 2** and wait until you reach the main menu or are fully in-game.

---

### Step 4 – Inject into Process

In Fate Injector:

* Select **Custom Target**
* Choose: `rdr2.exe`

**Note:** Ensure **DirectX is actively running** (the game must be fully initialized).

---

### Step 5 – Open Menu

* The menu should appear automatically after injection
* If not, press:

  * `Insert` key
  * or `Shift + 0` (alternate Insert binding)
